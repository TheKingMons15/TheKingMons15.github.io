2nd INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON APPLIED AND COMPUTATIONAL MATHEMATICS - ICACM S 2024

DATES: Wednesday 29th, Thursday 30th, and Friday 31st of May 2024

About Us

The ICACM 2024S aims to provide a main forum for students in applied mathematics and computer science to exchange ideas, discuss trends and challenges in these fields, present their latest developments and research, and establish contacts for future collaborations.

Organizers

We appreciate the universities that collaborated in the planning and execution of this project.
